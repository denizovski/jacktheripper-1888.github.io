Metadata Alignment

We researched and defined the standards used by institutions. Then we matched its features according to their roles and made the standards (EDM, DCMI Metadata Requirements, EAD, ISBD, IPTC) compatible. Then, we divided the features into four main categories (Person, Date, Place, Event). It is possible to notice that the tables have some materials and objects, but conversely, each object has its own standard.
